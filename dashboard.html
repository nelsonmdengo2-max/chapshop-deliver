Help & Support

Find answers or reach out to us.

How do I create an order?

Go to "Create Order" from the sidebar menu. Fill in the recipient's name, phone, address, and city. Add the COD amount if applicable, then click Submit.

What does COD mean?

COD stands for Cash On Delivery. It's the amount the customer will pay the delivery rider when they receive their package.

How do I track my orders?

Go to "All Orders" to see a list of all your orders with their current status: Pending, Picked, Delivered, or Returned.

Can I change an order's status?

Yes! Go to "All Orders", find the order, and click the status badge to change it.
