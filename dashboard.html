Help & Support

Find answers or reach out to us.

How do I create an order?

Go to "Create Order" from the sidebar menu. Fill in the recipient details and click Submit.

What does COD mean?

Cash On Delivery — the amount the customer pays when they receive their package.

How do I track my orders?

Go to "All Orders" to see every order with its current status. You can also filter by status.
